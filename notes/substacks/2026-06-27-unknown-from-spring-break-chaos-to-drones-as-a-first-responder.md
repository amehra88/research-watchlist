### Chief Wayne Jones of the Miami Beach Police Department

Miami Beach Police Chief Wayne Jones joins Mike Wagers to discuss one of America's most unique policing environments. From managing massive spring break crowds and combating human trafficking to pioneering drones as first responders, Chief Jones shares how technology, partnerships, and proactive policing are helping keep residents and visitors safe in one of the world's most recognizable tourist destinations, Miami Beach, FL.
