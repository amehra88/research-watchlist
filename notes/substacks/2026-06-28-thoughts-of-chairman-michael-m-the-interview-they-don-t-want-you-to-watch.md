# [The interview they don't want you to watch?](https://substack.com/app-link/post?publication_id=2073517&post_id=203939189&utm_source=post-email-title&utm_campaign=email-post-title&isFreemail=true&r=8ne7oq&token=eyJ1c2VyX2lkIjo1MjMwMjM3MjIsInBvc3RfaWQiOjIwMzkzOTE4OSwiaWF0IjoxNzgyNjM5MDY2LCJleHAiOjE3ODUyMzEwNjYsImlzcyI6InB1Yi0yMDczNTE3Iiwic3ViIjoicG9zdC1yZWFjdGlvbiJ9.TIJgE_5PRrn3VzhkL65QQHP_QJ31SrjyCw3oW9pd73M)

### Former U.S. Secretary of State John Kerry negotiated the JCPOA and the Paris Agreement. Here he is very critical of President Trump - and YouTube seems to have shadow-banned the conversation.

Former Secretary of State John Kerry negotiated both the JCPOA deal with Iran that President Trump tore up, and the Paris Agreement, which the US has left under not one but two Trump administrations.

President Trump is now struggling to seal a new long-term deal with Iran. It seems highly unlikely he will achieve anything better the JCPOA (perhaps with the exception of mixing in a $300 billion real estate fund), as Kerry explains.

The episode is fascinating - Kerry does not mince his words. It is performing well on podcast platforms, but is so far one of our worst-performing episodes of Cleaning Up on YouTube. Why is that?

We know YouTube shadow-bans content that it deems political advertising. They did it to [the episode we filmed on Project Bo](https://substack.com/redirect/2294d7fc-8868-4ea0-a222-54d4e8c69b8d?j=eyJ1IjoiOG5lN29xIn0.bqmJ9TjykdtTXCviJ3jD2X5vxhprRGd5tpCaC89FQIA), the solar and battery system a bunch of us funded and built in Africa - apparently saving babies’ lives during the week that Elon Musk was busy dismantling USAID was a political act.

Anyway, I urge you to watch my conversation with Kerry. Whatever you think of him politically, or as a former Presidential candidate, he was a towering figure in foreign relations over a period of decades.
