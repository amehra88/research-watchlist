Item 5.07. Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
On April 24, 2024, Entegris, Inc. (the "Company") held its 2024 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the "Annual Meeting"). As of March 8, 2024, the record date for the Annual Meeting, there were 150,733,837 shares of the Company's Common Stock issued and outstanding and entitled to vote on the matters presented at the Annual Meeting. Holders of 141,841,985 shares of the Company's Common Stock, or 94.1% of the outstanding shares entitled to be cast at the Annual Meeting, which constituted a quorum, were represented at the Annual Meeting in-person or by proxy.
