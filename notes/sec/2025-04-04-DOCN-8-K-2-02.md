Item 2.02 Results of Operations and Financial Condition
On April 4, 2025 DigitalOcean Holdings, Inc. (the “Company”) held an Investor Day during which the Company reaffirmed guidance for the fiscal quarter ended March 31, 2025 and the year ended December 31, 2025, as previously issued in the Company’s press release dated February 25, 2025 announcing the Company's financial results for the fiscal quarter and year ended December 31, 2024.
A replay of the webcast, along with the presentation materials, will be available on the Company’s investor relations website at http://investors.digitalocean.com until April 3, 2026 at 11:59PM ET.
