Item 7.01.
Regulation FD.
Photronics, Inc.’s (the “Registrant”) wholly-owned subsidiary in Cheonan, Korea experienced a power outage on August 15, 2022. Power at the facility was
restored within one day but the facility was not fully operational for nearly two weeks. While the facility is fully operational as of the date of this current report on Form 8-K, the loss of power will have a financial impact on the fourth
quarter. This financial impact was factored into our fourth quarter guidance.
The information furnished pursuant to this Item 7.01 shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the
“Exchange Act”), nor shall it be deemed incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, except as expressly set forth by specific reference in such a filing.
