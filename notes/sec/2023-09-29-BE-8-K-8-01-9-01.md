Item 8.01
Other Events.
On September 29, 2023, Bloom Energy Corporation (the “Company”) filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”) a prospectus supplement to the prospectus included in the Company’s registration statement on Form S-3ASR (File No. 333-260464), filed with the SEC on October 25, 2021 (the “Registration Statement”), covering the resale by SK ecoplant Co., Ltd. (“SK ecoplant”) and Econovation, LLC (“Econovation”), an affiliated entity of SK ecoplant of which SK ecoplant is the managing member, of up to 23,491,701 shares (the “Shares”) of the Company’s Class A common stock, par value $0.0001 per share (“Class A common stock”), consisting of (i) up to 10,000,000 shares of Class A common stock held by SK ecoplant and (ii) up to 13,491,701 shares of Class A common stock held by Econovation.
A copy of the legal opinion of Latham & Watkins LLP relating to the validity of the Shares is filed herewith as Exhibit 5.1 and is incorporated herein by reference, and is filed with reference to, and is hereby incorporated by reference into, the Registration Statement.
