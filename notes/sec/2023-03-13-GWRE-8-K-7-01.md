Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
Guidewire Software, Inc. (the "Company") is aware of reports indicating that Silicon Valley Bank ("SVB") has entered receivership. Of the Company's $870.0 million of cash, cash equivalents, and investments previously reported as of January 31, 2023, approximately 4% is held at SVB as of March 9, 2023. Thus, regardless of the ultimate outcome and the timing of ongoing developments related to SVB, the Company expects there will be no material impact on day-to-day operations.
