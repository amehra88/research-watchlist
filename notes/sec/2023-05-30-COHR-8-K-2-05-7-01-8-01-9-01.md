Item 2.05
Costs Associated with Exit or Disposal Activities.
On May 23, 2023, the Board of Directors of Coherent Corp. (the “Company”) approved the Company’s May 2023 Restructuring Plan which includes site consolidations, facilities moves and closures, as well as the relocation and requalification of certain manufacturing facilities. These restructuring actions will be accompanied by other cost reductions and are intended to realign the Company’s cost structure as part of a transformation to a simpler, more streamlined, resilient and sustainable business model.
The Company expects to incur approximately $150 million to $200 million of pre-tax charges primarily as a result of the reduction in force and facility consolidations related to the closure and relocation of sites. The charges include approximately $75 million to $100 million of cash-related severance and benefits charges. The charges also include approximately $75 million to $100 million of other expenses, which primarily consist of non-cash impairment charges for production assets and improvements on leased facilities.
The Company expects the restructuring actions to be substantially completed by the end of fiscal 2025. However, the actual timing and costs associated with these restructuring actions may differ from the Company’s current expectations and estimates and such differences may be material.
