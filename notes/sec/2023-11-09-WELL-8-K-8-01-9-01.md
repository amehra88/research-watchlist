Item 8.01
Other Events.
On November 9, 2023, Welltower Inc. (the “Company”) issued 17,500,000 shares of its common stock, par value $1.00 per share (the “Shares”), pursuant to an automatic shelf registration statement of the Company on Form S-3 (File No. 333-264093) filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”) on April 1, 2022, as amended by post-effective amendment no. 1 filed with the SEC on May 3, 2023. The Shares were sold pursuant to an Underwriting Agreement, dated as of November 6, 2023, among the Company, Welltower OP LLC, and BofA Securities, Inc. and Goldman Sachs & Co. LLC.
The foregoing description of certain terms of the Underwriting Agreement and the transaction contemplated thereby does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the terms and conditions of the Underwriting Agreement, which is filed herewith as Exhibit 1.1 and is incorporated by reference herein.
