Item 8.01.
Other Events
Notice of Redemption of Maxim Senior Notes
On March 24, 2023, Analog Devices, Inc. (the “Company”) gave notice that it will redeem for cash all of the outstanding 3.450% senior notes due June 15, 2027 issued by its wholly-owned subsidiary Maxim Integrated Products, Inc. (the “Maxim Notes”) in accordance with the terms of the indenture governing the Maxim Notes. The redemption date for the Maxim Notes will be April 26, 2023 (the “Redemption Date”) and the redemption price will be equal to the greater of: (a) 100% of the aggregate principal amount of the Maxim Notes, and (b) the sum of the present values of the remaining scheduled payments due on the Maxim Notes, discounted to the Redemption Date on a semi-annual basis (assuming a 360-day year consisting of twelve 30-day months) at the applicable treasury rate plus 20 basis points, plus accrued and unpaid interest thereon to the Redemption Date; plus, in each case, accrued and unpaid interest on the principal amount of the Maxim Notes to, but excluding, the Redemption Date. As of March 24, 2023, $59,788,000 aggregate principal amount of the Maxim Notes is outstanding. The Company intends to fund the aggregate redemption price using cash on hand.
