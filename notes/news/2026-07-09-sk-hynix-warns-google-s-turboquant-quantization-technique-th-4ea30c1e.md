## SK Hynix warns Google's TurboQuant quantization technique threatens HBM demand

Model-efficiency techniques reducing HBM consumption are a structural risk to the AI memory demand thesis; if TurboQuant achieves broad adoption, HBM volume assumptions for SK Hynix and peers may need to be revised downward.

- SK Hynix publicly flagged Google's TurboQuant as a potential headwind to HBM demand.
- TurboQuant is a model quantization method; if widely adopted, it could reduce memory bandwidth requirements in AI workloads.
- Warning originates from SK Hynix itself, lending credibility to the demand-risk signal.
