## AMD and Anthropic Sign Major Chips-and-Investment Deal Worth Up to $5 Billion

A committed up-to-$5B deal gives AMD a credible revenue anchor in AI compute and signals Anthropic is deliberately diversifying inference and training silicon away from NVDA exclusivity — a direct read on competitive intensity in the accelerator market heading into AMD's print.

- AMD and Anthropic have entered into a chips-and-investment deal valued at up to $5 billion, per a WSJ exclusive.
- AMD stock gained on the announcement; FactSet sentiment reads positive.
- AMD management publicly expressed satisfaction with the partnership.
- Deal spans both chip supply and an investment component, signaling a structural, not transactional, relationship.
- Aug. 4 earnings is the next formal catalyst where deal revenue impact may begin to be quantified.
