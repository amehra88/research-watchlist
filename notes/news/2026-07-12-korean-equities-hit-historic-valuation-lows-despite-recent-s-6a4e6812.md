## Korean Equities Hit Historic Valuation Lows Despite Recent Strong Performance

Historically cheap valuations in Korean equities — at the same time as strong fundamentals (near-80% DRAM margins, Yongin expansion) — could represent a structural discount or a rerating catalyst; for a PM long SK Hynix or Samsung, this is a key valuation tailwind to monitor.

- Bloomberg reports Korean stocks are trading at the cheapest valuations on record (per the headline).
- This follows a period of outperformance ('world-beating'), creating a valuation compression paradox.
- The discount directly affects benchmark Korean names including SK Hynix and Samsung.
- Medium confidence; cross-asset and positioning signal.
