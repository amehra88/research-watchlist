## Barron's argues SK Hynix's US listing will narrow its competitive gap with Micron in the memory market

- The HBM competitive landscape is the implied battleground; SK Hynix is currently a leading HBM supplier.
- Headline suggests the listing could structurally tighten the competition between 000660.KS and MU.
