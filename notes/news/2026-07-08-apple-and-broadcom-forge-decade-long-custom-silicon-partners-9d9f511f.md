## Apple and Broadcom Forge Decade-Long Custom Silicon Partnership

A decade-long anchor relationship substantially reduces volume uncertainty for Broadcom's custom silicon segment and deepens Apple's competitive moat against third-party chipmakers; for names on either side of this relationship, this locks in a key structural variable for an extended period.

- Apple and Broadcom have entered what the headline characterizes as a decade-long 'silicon fortress' arrangement.
- The framing implies deep custom chip design collaboration and a long-dated supply lock-in for both parties.
- Apple deepens its silicon vertical integration while Broadcom secures a long-term anchor customer relationship.
- The decade-long duration is notable for the revenue visibility it provides to Broadcom's Apple-facing business.
