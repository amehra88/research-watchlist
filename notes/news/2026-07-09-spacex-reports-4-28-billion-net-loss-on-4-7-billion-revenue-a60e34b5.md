## SpaceX Reports $4.28 Billion Net Loss on $4.7 Billion Revenue in Most Recent Quarter

This is the primary input for any serious SPCX underwriting.

- SpaceX disclosed a quarterly net loss of $4.28 billion against quarterly revenue of $4.7 billion
- The loss is approximately equal to the full revenue figure, indicating substantial cash consumption
- This is among the first major financial disclosures for the newly-public company
- Coverage is explaining the composition and rationale behind the outsized loss-to-revenue ratio
