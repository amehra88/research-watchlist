## Exclusive: SK Hynix in talks to acquire Intel's Ohio chip campus for US memory production

If completed, this reshuffles US memory production ownership and could accelerate SK Hynix's HBM manufacturing in a geopolitically favorable jurisdiction; for INTC it is a potential capital release from underutilized Ohio assets, with implications for the foundry capacity and supply-chain concentration debates.

- An exclusive report indicates SK Hynix (000660.KS) is in active talks to purchase Intel's Ohio fab campus
- The stated purpose is domestic US memory production—a direct semiconductor reshoring event
- Deal would be material for INTC as potential asset monetization and for SK Hynix as US HBM capacity footprint expansion
- Talks are ongoing and not a completed transaction; no deal value is specified in the headline
