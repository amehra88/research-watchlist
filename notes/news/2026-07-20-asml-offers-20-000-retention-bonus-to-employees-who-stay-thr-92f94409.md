## ASML Offers €20,000 Retention Bonus to Employees Who Stay Through 2030

- The five-year retention horizon signals management confidence in a sustained demand runway for lithography equipment
- Move reflects talent competition pressure in semiconductor equipment
- Long-dated retention program implies a multi-year capex cycle commitment rather than a near-term tactical play
