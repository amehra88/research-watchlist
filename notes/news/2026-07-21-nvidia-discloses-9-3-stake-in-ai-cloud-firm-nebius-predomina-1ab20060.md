## NVIDIA Discloses 9.3% Stake in AI Cloud Firm Nebius, Predominantly via March Warrant

NVIDIA validating Nebius as a preferred AI cloud partner via a near-$5B equity position elevates NBIS's strategic standing in the GPU-cloud ecosystem and flags where NVDA sees AI compute demand concentrating. The warrant-origin detail tempers the 'fresh conviction' read, but the public disclosure itself is a meaningful endorsement.

- NVIDIA disclosed a 9.3% ownership stake in Nebius Group (NBIS), valued at nearly $5B according to coverage.
- Approximately 95% of the stake derived from a March warrant, making this a disclosure of an existing position rather than a fresh open-market purchase.
- NBIS shares surged on the news; outlets differ on the magnitude (headlines cite both 7% and 12%).
- The stake signals a deepening strategic AI cloud partnership between NVIDIA and Nebius.
