## SK Hynix CEO warns 2027 will be the worst year in memory supply history

A CEO-level supply-crunch warning for 2027 is a pricing-power signal for all memory producers; sustained structural tightness through the AI demand ramp is a tailwind for MU and SK Hynix earnings, but it also raises HBM allocation risk for hyperscaler capex plans.

- SK Hynix CEO publicly characterized 2027 as the most severe memory supply crunch on record.
- The warning covers the broad memory market, implicating DRAM and HBM supply dynamics.
- Peers directly exposed include MU and Samsung (005930.KS) alongside SK Hynix (000660.KS).
- Signal is supply-side: constrained capacity — not demand collapse — is the cited driver.
