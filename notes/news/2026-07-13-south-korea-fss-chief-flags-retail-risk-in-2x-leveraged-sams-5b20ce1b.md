## South Korea FSS Chief Flags Retail Risk in 2x Leveraged Samsung and SK Hynix ETFs, No Concrete Action Announced

Regulatory attention on leveraged ETFs can dampen retail flows into these products; for SK Hynix specifically — which just launched its own leveraged ETF pair on Nasdaq — this adds a headline regulatory overhang around the trading vehicles tied to HBM names precisely as US retail interest is building.

- The head of South Korea's Financial Supervisory Service raised concerns about 2x leveraged ETFs tracking Samsung and SK Hynix.
- Coverage notes that specific regulatory solutions remain elusive — no concrete measure has been announced.
- The concern is centered on retail investor risk from leveraged products, not on the underlying businesses.
- This is a financial product regulatory signal, not a fundamental read on either company.
