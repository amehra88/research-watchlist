## Apple commits over $30 billion to Broadcom for chip supply

A $30B+ procurement anchor substantially de-risks AVGO's revenue outlook and confirms AAPL's commitment to vertically integrating silicon — relevant for both names and for tracking how hyperscaler/OEM custom-chip spending is flowing to fabless incumbents rather than pure foundries.

- Apple is investing more than $30 billion in Broadcom chips, per GuruFocus coverage — the third outlet to confirm the deal, reinforcing its scale
- Commitment spans chip design and supply, deepening the AAPL–AVGO silicon partnership
- Deal is material enough to draw sustained multi-outlet coverage, signaling strategic rather than routine procurement significance
- Positive revenue visibility event for AVGO; underscores AAPL's custom silicon push
