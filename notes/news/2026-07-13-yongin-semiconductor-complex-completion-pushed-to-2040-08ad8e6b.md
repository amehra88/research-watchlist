## Yongin Semiconductor Complex completion pushed to 2040

A Yongin delay to 2040 removes meaningful incremental Korean fab capacity from the near-to-medium term supply picture, which reduces competitive pressure on TSMC and may tighten advanced packaging supply chains underpinning AI infrastructure buildout.

- Korean reporting (Chosun Ilbo) indicates the Yongin semiconductor complex's completion has been pushed to 2040.
- The complex involves Samsung Electronics (005930.KS) and SK Hynix (000660.KS).
- No specific reason for the delay is cited in the headline.
- The revised timeline represents a significant slip in domestic Korean foundry and advanced semiconductor capacity.
