## Meta's in-house Iris AI chip enters production in September; company targets doubling compute capacity to 14 GW

- Meta confirmed its custom Iris AI chip will enter production in September 2026
- The chip ramp is part of a plan to double Meta's total computing capacity to 14 GW
- Signals accelerating silicon independence from merchant AI accelerator suppliers
- 14 GW compute target represents a major infrastructure commitment
- Directly relevant to custom-silicon vs. merchant-chip competitive dynamics
