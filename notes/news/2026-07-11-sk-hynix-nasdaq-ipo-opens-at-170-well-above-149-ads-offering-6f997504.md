## SK Hynix Nasdaq IPO Opens at $170, Well Above $149 ADS Offering Price

A strong SK Hynix open validates institutional appetite for HBM-exposed memory equity at scale; the US listing increases comparability with MU and other DRAM peers and will raise the bar on HBM pricing and volume disclosure expectations across the group.

- SK Hynix's Nasdaq IPO — a 177.9 million ADS offering — was priced at $149 per ADS.
- The stock opened at $170, a strong first-day pop above the offering price.
- SK Hynix is the leading HBM supplier, making this a landmark capital market event for the memory and AI-infrastructure sector.
- FactSet sentiment is Neutral, suggesting the move was largely anticipated in institutional coverage.
