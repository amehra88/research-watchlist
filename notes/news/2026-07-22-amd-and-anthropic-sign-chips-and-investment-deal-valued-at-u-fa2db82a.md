## AMD and Anthropic Sign Chips-and-Investment Deal Valued at Up to $5 Billion

- AMD and Anthropic announced a combined chip supply and investment deal; AMD will invest up to $5 billion in Anthropic as part of the arrangement.
- One headline (WSJ exclusive) characterizes the AI server supply component as 'tens of billions,' suggesting the investment and supply revenue are distinct elements of the overall structure.
- The deal is covered across four major outlets including a WSJ exclusive, indicating high sourcing confidence.
- The partnership validates AMD's AI server stack as a credible alternative to NVIDIA for frontier model compute.
