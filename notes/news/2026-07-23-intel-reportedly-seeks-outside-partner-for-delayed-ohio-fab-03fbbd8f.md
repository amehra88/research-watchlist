## Intel Reportedly Seeks Outside Partner for Delayed Ohio Fab; SK Hynix Named as Candidate

An Intel-SK Hynix Ohio partnership, if confirmed, would carry strategic implications for both foundry capacity diversification and HBM supply chain positioning — and would represent a meaningful shift in how Intel executes its domestic fab strategy. The pre-earnings stock lift suggests the market is reading this as optionality-positive for INTC.

- Reports indicate Intel is seeking an external partner for its delayed Ohio semiconductor facility.
- SK Hynix is named among the candidates under consideration.
- INTC shares ticked up ahead of earnings on the Ohio fab news.
- The development suggests Intel may be reconsidering or restructuring the fab's ownership or operating model.
