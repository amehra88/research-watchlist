## Morgan Stanley sees upside in Apple ahead of potential iPhone price increase

If the ASP expansion thesis is correct, an iPhone price increase would represent a near-term earnings and margin catalyst. The key risk is that higher pricing pressures upgrade cycles in a competitive global handset market, particularly in China.

- Morgan Stanley issues a bullish view on AAPL tied to a potential iPhone price increase.
- Thesis centers on average selling price expansion as evidence of handset pricing power.
- Headline is a single sell-side opinion; no specific price target, timing, or pricing magnitude is cited.
- Directional read is positive for AAPL, contingent on a price increase materializing.
