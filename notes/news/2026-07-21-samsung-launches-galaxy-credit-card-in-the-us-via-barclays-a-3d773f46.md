## Samsung Launches Galaxy Credit Card in the US via Barclays and Visa, Targeting Apple Card

Samsung entering US financial services with a competitive cashback card is a directional encroachment on the Apple ecosystem's financial stickiness; at scale, Android-native financial products could erode switching-cost moat supporting Apple's services revenue, though near-term impact is likely limited.

- Samsung launched the Galaxy Card in the US, issued through Barclays and operating on the Visa network.
- The card offers up to 5% cash-back rewards and a $200 sign-up bonus.
- Samsung explicitly positions the product as a rival to Apple Card, broadening its financial-services ecosystem in the US.
