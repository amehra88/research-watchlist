## Court Upholds $634M Verdict Against Apple in Masimo Apple Watch Patent Dispute

A confirmed $634M liability is modest relative to Apple's cash position, but the ruling keeps alive broader IP risk around Apple Watch health-sensing; if Masimo pursues injunctive relief or further litigation, feature-set limitations on Apple Watch become a more material concern for the wearables growth thesis.

- A court upheld a $634M verdict in favor of Masimo in its patent dispute with Apple over Apple Watch.
- FactSet sentiment on the story is Neutral.
- AAPL shares were up modestly on the day despite the ruling, suggesting the market views the liability as manageable in isolation.
- The ruling is a setback for Apple's IP defense strategy around Apple Watch health-sensing features.
