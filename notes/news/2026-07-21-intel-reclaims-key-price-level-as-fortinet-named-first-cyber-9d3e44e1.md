## Intel Reclaims Key Price Level as Fortinet Named First Cybersecurity Customer for Intel Foundry Services

Fortinet as the first named cybersecurity IFS customer is a meaningful proof-point that Intel's foundry pivot is attracting non-traditional logic customers — if IFS can anchor security and specialized workloads, it diversifies INTC's foundry revenue base and strengthens the thesis that IFS is a real strategic alternative to TSMC for certain customer segments.

- INTC shares reclaimed a key technical level of $100, per the FXLeaders headline.
- Fortinet has been identified as the first cybersecurity company to become a foundry customer under Intel Foundry Services (IFS), marking a concrete milestone for INTC's foundry-as-a-service strategy.
- This is a named, specific customer win — not speculative — adding credibility to IFS's commercial viability beyond commodity logic manufacturing.
- No wafer volume, financial terms, or chip program details are disclosed.
