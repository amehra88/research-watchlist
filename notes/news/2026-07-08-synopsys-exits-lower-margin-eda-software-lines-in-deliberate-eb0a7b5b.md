## Synopsys Exits Lower-Margin EDA Software Lines in Deliberate Shift to Higher-Margin Products

SNPS proactively culling low-margin EDA lines signals confidence in pricing power at the high end of the tool stack — bullish for SNPS margins, but raises the second-order question of who captures the vacated lower-tier seats and whether that hardens or erodes the EDA duopoly's competitive moat.

- Synopsys is discontinuing certain EDA software products sold to chipmakers, per Reuters.
- The rationale is an explicit product-mix shift toward higher-margin offerings.
- FactSet sentiment on the story is neutral.
- This is a voluntary strategic exit, not a competitive loss — Synopsys is choosing to leave these lines rather than being displaced.
