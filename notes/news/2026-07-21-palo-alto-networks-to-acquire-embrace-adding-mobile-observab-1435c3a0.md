## Palo Alto Networks to Acquire Embrace, Adding Mobile Observability to Its Platform

The Embrace deal extends PANW's platform consolidation strategy into mobile observability, increasing competitive overlap with pure-play monitoring vendors and deepening enterprise lock-in — a directionally positive read for the platformization thesis, though deal size is unconfirmed.

- PANW announced the acquisition of Embrace, described as an observability company focused on the mobile layer.
- The deal is framed as expanding PANW's platform into mobile observability — an adjacent capability to its core security offering.
- This is an M&A-driven platform extension, not an organic product launch.
- No deal terms are disclosed in coverage.
