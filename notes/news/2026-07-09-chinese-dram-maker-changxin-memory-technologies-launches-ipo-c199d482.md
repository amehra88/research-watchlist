## Chinese DRAM maker ChangXin Memory Technologies launches IPO targeting 6.5 trillion Won

A well-capitalized Chinese DRAM entrant accelerates domestic capacity additions and could pressure pricing for Western and Korean incumbents over the medium term; PMs should monitor whether CXMT's ramp compresses commodity DRAM margins before HBM-driven mix shift fully offsets the pressure.

- CXMT has begun its IPO process with a stated fundraising target of 6.5 trillion Won.
- The listing signals China is channeling significant capital into domestic DRAM capacity.
- The raise introduces a competitive threat to incumbent memory players including MU, SK Hynix, and Samsung.
- The IPO carries a geopolitical dimension as part of China's broader semiconductor self-sufficiency drive.
