## Intel and Fortinet Announce Joint Development of Next-Generation Security Processor 6

A silicon-level cybersecurity partnership is Intel's most credible near-term diversification move given DCAI weakness; for FTNT, a dedicated next-gen security processor could be a differentiation lever in a crowded firewall market worth tracking into the product cycle.

- Intel and Fortinet entered a strategic collaboration to co-develop a next-generation security chip designated Security Processor 6.
- The partnership represents Intel pivoting its silicon capabilities toward cybersecurity as a growth vector, partially offsetting DCAI retrenchment.
- FactSet sentiment: Positive, consistent with the market reading this as a credible strategic broadening for Intel.
