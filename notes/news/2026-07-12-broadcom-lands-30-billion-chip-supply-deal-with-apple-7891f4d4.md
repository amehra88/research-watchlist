## Broadcom Lands $30 Billion Chip Supply Deal With Apple

A $30B commitment from Apple is a material, long-duration revenue visibility event for AVGO and a concrete data point that custom silicon demand from hyperscalers and consumer tech leaders remains intact at scale; for AAPL it confirms continued investment in proprietary silicon as a strategic moat.

- Broadcom has secured a chip deal with Apple valued at $30 billion, per the headline.
- Deal is framed as a win for both parties: revenue backlog visibility for AVGO, custom silicon supply security for AAPL.
- Reinforces Apple's vertical integration strategy and reduces its dependence on third-party chip designs.
- Significant revenue commitment signal for AVGO's custom ASIC pipeline.
