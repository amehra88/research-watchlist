# AI Will Create More Delivery Jobs

Here are the investment insights from the podcast clip, tailored for a public markets tech/AI analyst.

**Public Company Commentary: DoorDash (DASH)**

*   **Bullish Growth Metrics:** A DoorDash co-founder states the business is currently growing at 25% year-over-year.
*   **Long-Term Valuation Ambition:** The company is planning for a future where the business is 5x to 10x its current size in a 10-year timeframe.
*   **Operating Constraint:** At that scale, the primary bottleneck will be the supply of human labor ("Dashers"). The current model is not scalable to 5-10x without significant changes.

**AI/Tech Sector Trend: Labor & Automation in Logistics**

*   **Automation is Additive, Not Substitutive:** The core thesis is that for high-growth delivery platforms, AI, robotics, and drones will not replace human workers in the next decade. Instead, both will be necessary to meet overwhelming demand growth.
*   **Human Labor Demand to Increase:** Despite the introduction of robotics and AI, the speaker explicitly predicts there will be "more Dashers doing deliveries, not less" in 10 years because the overall market is expanding so rapidly.

**Capital Allocation & Strategy**

*   **"Multimodal Fleet" Strategy:** DoorDash is planning for a future that combines human drivers with automated solutions like robotics and drones.
*   **Investment Signal:** The company will need to "get our hands on every single modality we can get." This signals a strong imperative for investment, partnerships, and/or M&A in the robotics and autonomous vehicle space to augment their human network and achieve long-term growth targets.
