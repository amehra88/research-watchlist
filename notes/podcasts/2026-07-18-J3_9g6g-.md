# David Friedberg: Scientists just REVERSED skin age from 70 years to 31

Here are the investing insights from the podcast episode.

### AI/Tech Sector Trends & Market Commentary

*   **Trillion-Dollar Market Potential:** The hosts call the market for a successful topical anti-aging treatment a "trillion-dollar revenue market" and later "a $2 trillion market." This highlights the massive commercial opportunity for companies that can successfully translate this type of biotech breakthrough into a consumer product.
*   **Convergence of AI and Synthetic Biology:** The core technological trend is the combination of AI-powered protein structure prediction (like AlphaFold), directed evolution (engineering novel proteins), and high-throughput screening. This combination allows for the creation of novel enzymes that don't exist in nature to solve specific biological problems, such as aging. This is a powerful new paradigm for drug and therapeutic discovery.

### Specific Public Company Mentions

*   **Alphabet (GOOGL):** The breakthrough relied on a combination of technologies, explicitly including **AlphaFold** (1:15). AlphaFold is an AI system from Google's DeepMind subsidiary. This is a bullish signal for Alphabet, demonstrating a real-world, high-value application of its foundational AI research in creating potentially trillion-dollar markets in biotechnology. It validates the long-term value of its R&D investments beyond its core advertising business.
